Privacy Policy for Wordlock

Last updated: {{MONTH}} {{YEAR}}

Wordlock (“the App”) is designed to help parents create positive screen-time routines by encouraging children to spell a word before opening selected apps. We value your and your child’s privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

⸻

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App does not request or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, birthdays, or any personally identifiable information.

The App does not use: • User accounts • Login systems • Cloud storage • Social media integrations • Third-party analytics • Cookies • Advertising identifiers (such as AAID or IDFA)

All word selection, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

⸻

Data Shared With Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not use advertising networks. We do not sell or rent any data to anyone at any time.

⸻

Children’s Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Wordlock is designed for children under age 13 and complies with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by: • Collecting no personal information • Using no identifiers to track users • Including a parent-only access section protected by a PIN or similar control

⸻

Parental Controls

The App includes a protected settings area intended only for parents. Please ensure that parental access controls remain secure.

⸻

Security

All customization data stays on your device. If you delete the App, this information is permanently removed.

⸻

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy occasionally. Updated versions will be posted on the same page.
